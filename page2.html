pancakes



ingredients

  • Japanese hot cake mix (150g)
  • Egg (1)
  • Milk (100mL)

instructions

  1. Put egg and milk in bowl and mix well
  2. Put the hot cake mix in the bowl with the egg and milk and mix lightly
  3. Put frying pan on stove and put on mid heat until it gets hot
  4. After the frying pan gets hot, turn off the heat
  5. Take â…“ of the pancake batter and put onto frying pan (pour on around 30cm above frying pan)
  6. Let the pancake cook until the batter bubbles on top then flip and let cook until golden brown
  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 until you have used all of your batter
  8. Serve with preferred toppings
