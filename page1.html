soba



ingredients

  • Water (1L)
  • Water (150mL) [for different purpose]
  • Japanese buckwheat noodles (1 bundle)
  • Japanese soup base for noodles or mentsuyu (50mL)

instructions

  1. Boil 1L of water in big pot
  2. Put noodles in and stir
  3. When the water comes to a rapid boil, decrease heat and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Put soba in strainer and rinse
  5. Drain water after rinsing
  6. Mix mentsuyu and 150mL water in a bowl
  7. Serve noodles in separate bowl
  8. Dip noodles in soup base as you eat
