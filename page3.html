curry



ingredients

  • Meat (1 lb)
  • Chopped up Onions (1.3 lbs)
  • Chopped up Carrots (0.5 lbs)
  • Chopped up Potatoes (1 lb)
  • Water (6 cups)
  • S&B TASTY CURRY (one box)
  • Oil (2 tbsp)

instructions

  1. Put all meat in vegetables in a big pot and stir fry with oil until cooked
  2. Add water and make it boil and let sit for 20 min
  3. Turn off heat
  4. Curry base into pot and mix until all of the pieces are dissolved
  5. Turn on heat at medium temperature
  6. Let sit for at least 5 to 15 min
  7. Serve with rice, bread or by itself
